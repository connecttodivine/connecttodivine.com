Agriculture Experiment


Impact of Energy Transmissions on Growth of Various Plants
Years of experiment: 2013 & 2014

Place of Experiment:
Wheeling, Illinois, USA

Treated Plants:
Sunflower
Squash
Tomato
Melon
Brussels Sprout
Pumpkin

Description of Experiment:
In 2013 and 2014 various plants were treated with Aksana Hancharuk’s biofield energy. The results can be seen in the slides, with examples of control (not treated) samples and samples treated with Divine Energy, side by side. The effect of energy transmissions can be observed in both roots ans stems of the plants. Both Brussels sprout plants were treated with energy, the pictures were taken late November after a few days with temperature below freezing.
